Our Story

Founded in late 2023, EuclideanLabs is a family-run business born from a shared passion for creativity, technology, and craftsmanship. What began as a hobby in a garage has rapidly evolved into a dynamic 3D printing and CAD design company. Serving a diverse clientele, from hobbyists to small businesses, we take pride in transforming innovative ideas into tangible realities with precision and care.

At EuclideanLabs, we harness the power of additive manufacturing to address real-world challenges. Whether crafting a custom prototype, a unique cosplay piece, or a small-batch production run, every project is approached with enthusiasm and meticulous attention to detail. Our family team infuses each creation with a personal touch, ensuring solutions are tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients.

In just 18 months, EuclideanLabs has built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality, innovative designs. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of what’s possible with 3D printing, fostering creativity, and providing exceptional service. Our mission remains simple: to bring ideas to life with the precision and dedication that only a family-run business can offer.
